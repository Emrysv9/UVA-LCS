UVA LCS

University of Virginia's In-House League of Legends Tournament

About the Tournament

UVA LCS is a student-run League of Legends tournament designed to promote competitive gaming and community engagement at the University of Virginia. Matches are played weekly and streamed live with full stat tracking and analysis.

Schedule

Matches are held every Friday at 7 PM EST. Playoffs begin in November.
